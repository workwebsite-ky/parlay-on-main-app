About Parlay On Main

A restaurant and bar built on three words: eat, drink, socialize. This is the corner of Main Street where Racine comes together.

240 Main St, Racine
Where it started

A full kitchen and a real bar, under one roof

Parlay On Main was created to give Racine a place that does both sides right — a scratch kitchen turning out soul food, catfish and smash burgers, and a cocktail bar shaking flights, punches and orchid-topped martinis to order.

Hand-painted murals, bubble-glass chandeliers and tufted leather booths set the tone. It’s upscale without the attitude — a grown-and-sexy room where the food is honest and the night is yours.

Billy Smith · Founder
From the founder

Built by Billy Smith

Parlay is the vision of founder Billy Smith — a spot that feels like the neighborhood and treats every guest like family. The goal was simple: great food, strong drinks, and a room where people actually want to stay and socialize.

“We wanted to build the place we’d want to hang out in — good plates, good pours, good people. That’s the whole parlay.”

House Policy

A safe, respectful, welcoming room for everyone

At Parlay On Main we’re committed to good vibes and the safety of every guest. We enforce a zero-tolerance policy for fighting, physical disturbances or violent behavior. Anyone engaging in such conduct will be removed from the premises, law enforcement will be contacted, and that individual will be permanently banned.

  • Respect our staff, our guests and our community.
  • Proper ID is required — select nights are 21+ or 30+.
  • Good vibes only, so everyone enjoys the night.

Thank you for your cooperation. — Management
