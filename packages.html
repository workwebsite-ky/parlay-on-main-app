Before you book

Good to know

How do I lock in a table?
Call Parlay at +1 262-456-0680 or send us a message through the contact page. A 50% deposit confirms your section for the night.
What’s the deposit policy?
All table packages require a 50% deposit upon booking. The balance and any additional bottles or bottles-on-arrival are settled at the venue.
Is there an age requirement?
Select nights are 21+ or 30+ — proper ID is always required. Check the event you’re booking, and bring a valid government ID for everyone in your party.
Can you host my birthday or private party?
Absolutely — birthdays and themed celebrations are our specialty. Reserve a section, add bottle service and we’ll coordinate the details. Visit the Events page for inspiration.
