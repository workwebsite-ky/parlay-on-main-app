The Room on Main
Welcome to Parlay

A neighborhood spot with grown & sexy energy

Parlay On Main blends a full-service kitchen with a real cocktail bar — orchid-topped martinis, margarita and lemon-drop flights, and a menu that runs from smash burgers and Philly cheesesteaks to deep-fried catfish and Sunday soul food.

Pull up to the bar, grab a tufted booth, or reserve a VIP section with bottle service. However you parlay, the vibe stays the same: good food, strong pours, better company.
